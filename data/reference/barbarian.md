# Barbarian (Levels 1-2) — 2024 PHB Reference

## Hit Points
- **Hit Dice:** 1d12 per level
- **HP at 1st Level:** 12 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels:** 1d12 (or 7) + CON modifier per level

## Proficiencies
- **Armor:** Light armor, medium armor, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, martial weapons
- **Saving Throws:** Strength, Constitution

## Level 1 Features

### Rage (2/long rest at level 1)
- **Bonus Action** to enter rage (lasts 10 rounds / 1 minute)
- Advantage on STR checks and STR saving throws
- +2 bonus to melee weapon damage (Rage Damage)
- Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage
- Can't cast spells or concentrate while raging
- Rage ends early if you don't attack or take damage since your last turn (2024: or use Bonus Action to maintain)

### Unarmored Defense
- AC = 10 + DEX modifier + CON modifier (when not wearing armor, can use shield)

### Weapon Mastery
- Choose 2 weapon mastery properties you can use

## Level 2 Features

### Danger Sense
- Advantage on DEX saving throws against effects you can see (traps, spells, etc.)
- Not relevant for basic melee combat sim

### Reckless Attack
- On your first attack of the turn, choose to attack recklessly
- You gain advantage on all melee STR-based attacks this turn
- Attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn

## Subclass: Path of the Berserker (Level 3+)
- Not available at level 2, but noting for future reference

## Notes (2024 changes)
- Rage can be maintained with Bonus Action (new)
- Weapon Mastery is new — barbarians get 2 slots at level 1
- Rage damage is still +2 at levels 1-8
