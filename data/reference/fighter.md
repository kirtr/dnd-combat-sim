# Fighter (Levels 1-2) — 2024 PHB Reference

## Hit Points
- **Hit Dice:** 1d10 per level
- **HP at 1st Level:** 10 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels:** 1d10 (or 6) + CON modifier per level

## Proficiencies
- **Armor:** All armor, shields
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, martial weapons
- **Saving Throws:** Strength, Constitution
- **Skills:** Choose 2 from Acrobatics, Animal Handling, Athletics, History, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, Survival

## Level 1 Features

### Fighting Style
Choose one:
- **Archery:** +2 to attack rolls with ranged weapons
- **Defense:** +1 AC while wearing armor
- **Dueling:** +2 damage with one-handed melee weapon (no other weapon in hand)
- **Great Weapon Fighting:** Reroll 1s and 2s on damage dice for two-handed/versatile weapons (must use new roll)
- **Two-Weapon Fighting:** Add ability modifier to off-hand damage

### Second Wind (1/short rest)
- **Bonus Action:** Regain 1d10 + fighter level HP
- Recharges on Short or Long Rest

### Weapon Mastery
- Choose 3 weapon mastery properties you can use

## Level 2 Features

### Action Surge (1/short rest)
- Take one additional action on your turn
- Recharges on Short or Long Rest

## Notes (2024 changes)
- Weapon Mastery is new in 2024 — fighters get 3 mastery slots at level 1
- Fighting styles are largely the same but are now considered feats in some contexts
- Second Wind is now explicitly a Bonus Action (was already, but clarified)
