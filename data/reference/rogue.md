# Rogue (Levels 1-2) — 2024 PHB Reference

## Hit Points
- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per level
- **HP at 1st Level:** 8 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + CON modifier per level

## Proficiencies
- **Armor:** Light armor
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, hand crossbow, longsword, rapier, shortsword
- **Saving Throws:** Dexterity, Intelligence

## Level 1 Features

### Expertise
- Double proficiency bonus for 2 chosen skill proficiencies
- Not combat-relevant for this sim

### Sneak Attack (1/turn)
- Extra 1d6 damage at levels 1-2
- Conditions: must use finesse or ranged weapon AND either:
  - Have advantage on the attack roll, OR
  - An ally is within 5ft of the target (and you don't have disadvantage)
- Once per turn (not once per round — can proc on opportunity attacks)

### Thieves' Cant
- Not combat-relevant

### Weapon Mastery
- Choose 2 weapon mastery properties you can use

## Level 2 Features

### Cunning Action
- **Bonus Action:** Dash, Disengage, or Hide
- Key tactical option: Disengage after attacking to avoid retaliation

## Notes (2024 changes)
- Sneak Attack is still 1d6 at level 1, scaling at odd levels
- Weapon Mastery is new — rogues get 2 slots at level 1
- Nick property on light weapons effectively gives rogues an extra attack in some cases
- Cunning Action remains the same
- In 1v1, Sneak Attack requires advantage (no allies nearby)
  - Assumption: Hide action via Cunning Action can grant advantage on next turn's attack
