# Monk (Levels 1-2) — 2024 PHB Reference

## Hit Points
- **Hit Dice:** 1d8 per level
- **HP at 1st Level:** 8 + CON modifier
- **HP at Higher Levels:** 1d8 (or 5) + CON modifier per level

## Proficiencies
- **Armor:** None
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons, martial weapons with Light property
- **Saving Throws:** Strength, Dexterity

## Level 1 Features

### Martial Arts
- Use DEX instead of STR for unarmed strikes and monk weapons (simple + light martial)
- Unarmed strike damage: 1d6 (replaces normal 1 + STR)
- **Bonus Action:** Make one unarmed strike after taking Attack action with unarmed/monk weapon

### Unarmored Defense
- AC = 10 + DEX modifier + WIS modifier (when not wearing armor, no shield)

### Weapon Mastery
- Choose 2 weapon mastery properties you can use

## Level 2 Features

### Monk's Focus (Ki → Focus Points in 2024)
- 2 Focus Points at level 2 (= monk level)
- Regain all on Short or Long Rest

### Flurry of Blows (1 Focus Point)
- After Attack action, spend 1 Focus Point as Bonus Action
- Make 2 unarmed strikes instead of 1 (replaces Martial Arts bonus attack)

### Patient Defense (1 Focus Point)
- Spend 1 Focus Point as Bonus Action
- Take the Dodge action

### Step of the Wind (1 Focus Point)
- Spend 1 Focus Point as Bonus Action
- Take Disengage or Dash action, jump distance doubled

### Unarmored Movement
- Speed increases by 10 ft while not wearing armor (+10 at level 2)

## Notes (2024 changes)
- Ki renamed to "Focus Points" in 2024
- Martial Arts die is 1d6 at level 1 (was 1d4 in 2014)
- Monk weapons now include light martial weapons
- Unarmed strikes use d6 from level 1
